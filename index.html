Relation to Development Studies

Intro

Research ethics promote the use of rigorous methodologies, data collection techniques, and analysis. Adhering to ethical standards helps to ensure that research findings are accurate, valid, and reliable.

Signfce

Development studies often involve working in diverse cultural contexts. Research ethics require researchers to respect and consider the cultural values, beliefs, and customs of the communities they are studying.

Ethics

Ethical research practices require researchers to maintain a high level of transparency and accountability throughout the research process.
